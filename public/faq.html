  • What types of yachts are available for charter in Ibiza?
    Our fleet features a diverse range of luxury yachts – from modern motor yachts to classic sailing vessels – each tailored to provide an exceptional experience in the Mediterranean.
  • What is included in a typical charter package?
    Every charter package includes a professional crew, comprehensive insurance, courtesy drinks, and a customised itinerary designed to showcase the best of Ibiza’s coastline and hidden coves.
  • Can I customise my charter itinerary in Ibiza?
    Yes, we offer fully bespoke itineraries. Whether you’re interested in exploring secluded beaches or enjoying Ibiza’s vibrant nightlife, our experts will tailor the experience to your preferences.
  • How do I book a yacht charter?
    Booking is straightforward – simply complete our online reservation form or contact our customer service team, who will guide you through the process and secure your charter.
  • What is your payment policy?
    We require a 50% deposit at the time of booking to secure your yacht charter. The remaining balance is due on the day of the charter. We accept bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and cash payments.
  • What is your cancellation policy?
    Our cancellation policy varies by season and yacht type. Please refer to our terms and conditions or contact our support team for detailed information on cancellations and refunds.
  • Can I organise private events or corporate charters on a yacht?
    Absolutely. We specialise in bespoke private events and corporate charters. Contact us with your requirements, and we’ll design an unforgettable experience tailored just for you.
  • Are food and drinks included in the charter?
    We offer complimentary drinks on board. Additional catering options are available upon request. You can also pre-order from our selection of gourmet menus.
  • Do I need a boating license to charter a yacht?
    No, our yacht charters include a professional captain and crew, ensuring a smooth and luxurious experience for our guests.
  • What should I bring for my yacht charter?
    We recommend bringing sunscreen, swimwear, sunglasses, and any personal items you may need. Towels and safety equipment are provided on board.
