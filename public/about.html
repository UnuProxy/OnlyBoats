About Us

Premium Charter Experience
Discover Ibiza's Waters in Style

Welcome to Just Enjoy Ibiza Boats, your premier luxury yacht charter service in the Balearic Islands. We specialize in creating unforgettable maritime experiences, combining pristine vessels with exceptional service to explore the crystal-clear waters of Ibiza and Formentera.

Our fleet features carefully selected luxury yachts, each maintained to the highest standards and crewed by experienced professionals. Whether you're planning a romantic sunset cruise, a family day trip to Formentera, or an exclusive corporate event, we ensure every journey exceeds expectations.
